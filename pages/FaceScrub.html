Face Scrub

A face scrub is a skincare product that removes dead skin cells and unclogs pores. It can also help even out skin tone and make skin look healthier.

How Its Work

  • Face scrubs contain a physical abrasive, such as crushed shells or powdered fruit seeds.
  • The abrasive buffs away dead skin cells.
  • Some scrubs also contain cleansers, oils, or acids to remove dirt and oil.

Benifits

  • Face scrubs can help even out skin tone
  • They can help remove dirt and oil
  • They can help unclog pores
  • They can help reveal skin with better texture and clarity

How to Use

  • Use a face scrub once or twice a week.
  • Scrubbing too much can damage your skin.
  • You can follow up with a hydrating night cream after using a face scrub at night.

What to Avoid

  • Avoid over-exfoliating, which can damage your skin's protective layer.
  • Avoid scrubs that contain creams that can block pores and cause whiteheads.

How to make your own Scrub

  • You can make your own face scrub by mixing sugar and honey.
