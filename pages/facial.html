Face Cleanup

A face cleanup is a process that removes dirt, dead skin cells, and other impurities from the face. It can also help prevent acne and make skin smoother and clearer.

Steps in a face cleanup

  1. Remove makeup: Use a makeup remover or cleansing oil
  2. Cleanse: Use a gentle face wash to remove dirt and impurities
  3. Exfoliate: Remove dead skin cells 1-2 times a week
  4. Tone: Balance the skin's pH and tighten pores

  7. Moisturize: Keep skin hydrated and protected
  8. Apply mask: Use a face mask that's suitable for your skin type

Tips for a face cleanup

  • Choose skincare products that are suitable for your skin type
  • Follow the steps in the correct order
  • Be gentle with your skin during the exfoliation and extraction steps
  • Avoid washing your face until it feels tight, as this can cause excess dryness
